About BS GRANULE

BS GRANULE is a miraculous fertilizer coated with 60 minerals and special vegetable amino acids.

It's a highly functional soil-improving granular fertilizer containing zeolite and pozzolan NPK.

The product excels at supplying nutrients for extended periods without losing active ingredients in sandy soil.

Beyond producing healthy and high-quality agricultural products, it also helps restore soil health.
