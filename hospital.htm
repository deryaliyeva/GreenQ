About Our Product

Chlorine Dioxide

Chlorine dioxide is an oxygen and chlorine compound with powerful effectiveness against bacteria, viruses, and microbes. It's internationally recognized by WHO, EPA, FDA, JECFA, USP, HACCP, and KFDA as a fully functional medical-grade disinfectant.

Operating Principle

Virus Zero penetrates cell walls and protein structures, completely eliminating viruses, bacteria, and fungi through oxidation. This unique mechanism ensures comprehensive sterilization across a wide range of pathogens.
